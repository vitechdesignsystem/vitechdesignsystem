Foundations

Foundations are the visual elements that we recommend and utilize to create engaging layouts and standardized, end-to-end, user experiences for our digital products.

  • Colors
    Colors are used to create consistent experiences across our products.
  • Typography
    Roboto is our default font to ensure that the UI is optimized and legible.
  • Icons
    Icons are visual representations of commands, devices, or actions.
  • Layout & Grid
    The grid is the foundation for laying out UI elements onscreen.
  • Spacings
    Spacings create a similar rhythm of how content flows across all screens.
  • Branding
    Our Vitech brand and digital product logos are impactful and recognizable.

Content

Our content guidelines elaborate our voice and tone, and the mechanics of our grammar and style. We recommend using accurate and conversational language to convey the messages our end-users need to know in order to clearly guide them to where they need to go.

  • Language & Grammar
    Use conventions to make your writing clear, consistent, no internal jargons.
  • UI Text
    Guidance for elements such as button labels, headings, form field labels, etc.
  • Voice & Tone
    Accurate, credible, conversational, and no technical jargons.
